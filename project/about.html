About ThermaCore

Protecting buildings and their occupants with quality fireproofing and insulation solutions.

Our Story

ThermaCore is a trusted applicator for fireproofing, insulation, and drywall systems across the Greater Toronto Area and surrounding regions. Our team of professionals brings decades of experience to every project, specializing in protecting commercial, industrial and residential properties with advanced fireproofing solutions, high-performance insulation, and expert drywall installation. We’re committed to delivering code-compliant results that prioritize safety, quality, and efficiency. Whether it’s fire-rated assemblies, energy-efficient insulation, or seamless drywall finishes, ThermaCore delivers lasting protection and peace of mind—because your safety is our top priority.
